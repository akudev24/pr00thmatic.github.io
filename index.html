(solo dev) (own music)

About me...

Hi! I am Ruth García AKA: pr00thmatic. I love gamedev! I started as a 2D artist in the GGJ2014 and then jumped right into javascript and phaser, the opportunity to work as a freelancer game developer came to me, and then all this opportunities required from me to learn Unity, and then Arduino, and then Playcanvas... I love learning to work with new engines and technology for any gamedev-related thing.

I have a programmer's heart, it's my favorite thing in the world, but I've always loved videogames even since I was a toddler, from playing them to making them, so that's why I also learned to be a generalist because it's very amazing to me all the process involved on making games.

So... by all means! please feel free to contact me in any social network to chat about game development.

And sorry if my syntax or grammar isn't correct, I am Bolivian/Guatemalan, and english is not my main language hehehe.
